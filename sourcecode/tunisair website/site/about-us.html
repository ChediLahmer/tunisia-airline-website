hi, we are Tunisair

Hello! We are Tunisair, the national airline of Tunisia, founded in 1948. We serve more than 120 destinations worldwide and have a fleet of over 30 aircraft. Tunisair operates regular and charter flights to meet the needs of passengers in terms of business travel, vacations, or pilgrimage. We also offer additional services such as car rental, accommodation, and guided tours to provide a complete travel experience for our customers. Tunisair is committed to the safety and comfort of its passengers and continually works to improve its services to meet the expectations of its customers.

Our Timeline

Tunisair has a long history as the national airline of Tunisia, serving destinations around the world since its founding in 1948. Over the years, the airline has expanded its operations, modernized its fleet, and faced various challenges and changes in the aviation industry.

1948
Tunisair is founded as the national airline of Tunisia.
1980s
Tunisair begins to modernize its fleet by introducing newer aircraft models, such as the Airbus A300.
2000s
Tunisair introduces new services, such as online booking and mobile check-in, and continues to modernize its fleet by adding newer aircraft models, such as the Boeing 737 and Airbus A319.
